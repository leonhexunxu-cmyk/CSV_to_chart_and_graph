Welcome to ChartCraft Pro v5!

The ultimate browser-based Office Suite — charts, presentations, and word processing.

New in v5: Full Word Editor, slide transitions, object animations, presenter mode, shapes, master slides, and find/replace.

No data on hand? Hit Sample Data in the top bar and explore right away.
📂

1 — Upload Data

Drag & drop or click the upload zone. Supports .csv, .xlsx, .xls, .numbers.

Enable Smart Parser for messy formats like:
Week 1: 45  ·  Q3 Sales - 89  ·  March 120
🎨

2 — Configure & Color

Choose a chart type, pick a palette, and set a title.

Single Color Mode: Use one color for the entire chart.
Gradient Mode: Apply gradient fills to bars, lines, donuts, and more.
🎬

3 — Presentation Editor

Open Presentation from the sidebar to create slide decks.

Layouts: Title, Title+Content, Two-Column, Comparison, Blank.
Shapes: Insert rectangles, circles, arrows, stars, and lines.
Animations: Add entrance animations to any object.
Transitions: Fade, Slide, Cube, Flip, Wipe, Dissolve.
📝

4 — Word Editor

Open Word Editor for full document editing with print layout.

Features: styles, find & replace, word count, comments, headers/footers, tables, and export to DOCX/PDF.
📧

5 — Share & Export

Connect your Gmail account to send directly.

Use Export as URL to copy a shareable link, or download PNG, PDF, HTML, PPTX.

Switch themes using the selector in the top bar.

ℹ️ About

ChartCraft Pro v5 — Office Suite

Built with Chart.js, PptxGenJS, jsPDF, html2canvas, and PapaParse. All data stays in your browser via localStorage.

Features: Charts, Presentations, Word Editor, Transitions, Animations, Shapes, Comments, Track Changes, Find & Replace, Gmail.
